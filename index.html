Cloud services are taking over the world and services like Dropbox, Google Drive and Ubuntu one have provided excellent services to store your data in the cloud. They even offer free storage up to 15 Gbs! However, one problem persists with using these cloud storage services to there full potential. How do I easily use all of these services together and take advantage of all that free cloud storage that is up for grabs? With Combicloud, that is made easy with a simple and intuitive app that integrates all of your cloud storage into one central location so that gigabytes upon gygabytes of free storage is made available in a well organized platform.
